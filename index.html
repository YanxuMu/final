Spring Restaurant

Welcome to Spring Restaurant, we are located in the world's most beautiful city Vancouver, in the heart of Great Vancouver Area, Burnaby, BC, Canada. We just opened in 2021, looking forward to the comments of tourists from all over the world.

Spring Restaurant's head chef is from Sichuan, another chef from Beijing Jianguomen Hotel. We are the authentic Chinese cuisine, covering Beijing, Shandong, Sichuan, Huaiyang Cuisine. We have a bright and spacious hall, which can accommodate 60 people for dinner and six 15 people large dining tables, fit for friends and family, good ideal for business gatherings. The restaurant has specialties, appetizers side dishes, seafood dishes, colored meat, seasonal vegetables, tofu dessert, colored dishes 150 balance. Specialties include Peking duck, Guo Bao Pork, Kung Pao Chicken, Cold Spicy Beef Offal, Sweet and Sour Fish.
